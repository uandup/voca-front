Level 3

Ambiguity

Synonyms

Vagueness Obscurity Equivocation

Definition & Part of Speech

Noun. 모호함, 다의성

The quality of being open to more than one interpretation; inexactness.

Example:

"The ambiguity of the poem allows multiple readings by different critics."

Level 4

Corroborate

Synonyms

Validate Verify Authenticate

Definition & Part of Speech

Verb. 확증하다, 입증하다

To confirm or give support to (a statement, theory, or finding).

Example:

"The witness was able to corroborate the suspect's alibi with specific details."

Level 2

Resilient

Synonyms

Tough Strong Adaptable

Definition & Part of Speech

Adjective. 회복력 있는, 탄력 있는

Able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions.

Example:

"Despite the heavy rains, the local crops proved remarkably resilient."
